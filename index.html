FAQ

Questions.

What driving licence do I need?+
The Horwin EK3 falls in the 125cc licence class. A standard car licence (Class B, held for at least 1 year) is enough. A motorcycle licence (A1, A2, or A) also works. We check at handover.
How does charging work?+
The battery is removable and charges from any standard 230V socket — just bring it up to your hotel room. Full charge takes about 5 hours overnight, giving you 80+ km.
Where do I pick up the scooter?+
We deliver to your hotel, Airbnb, or any central Munich meeting point. We walk you through the controls in about 5 minutes and you're off.
Is the scooter insured?+
Yes. The vehicle carries the required German third-party liability insurance (Kfz-Haftpflicht). The €200 deposit covers minor damage on your end. We recommend carrying personal travel insurance as well.
Can I ride outside Munich?+
Yes — that's half the point. The EK3 is fully road-legal across Germany. Starnberger See, Chiemsee, and Neuschwanstein are all within easy range on a single charge.
How do I pay?+
After we confirm your request, we send a secure payment link by email (Stripe or PayPal). The €200 deposit is handled at handover — cash or card — and returned on clean return.
What if something goes wrong during my rental?+
We're on WhatsApp throughout your rental. Mechanical issues we can't fix remotely result in a pro-rated refund for unused days.
