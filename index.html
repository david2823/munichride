FAQ

Common questions.

What licence do I need? +
The Horwin EK3 is classified as a 125cc-class vehicle. You need either a full car licence (Class B, held for 1+ year) or a motorcycle licence (A1, A2, or A). We check your licence at pickup — no licence, no ride.
How does charging work? +
The battery is fully removable and weighs about 15 kg. You can carry it to your hotel room and charge it from any standard 230V socket overnight. One charge takes roughly 5 hours and gives you 80+ km of range.
Where do I pick up the scooter? +
We bring the scooter to you — your hotel, Airbnb, or a central Munich meeting point. We walk you through the controls and you're off. No depot, no queues.
Is it insured? +
Yes. The vehicle is covered by liability insurance (Kfz-Haftpflicht) as required by German law. Your €200 security deposit covers minor damage. We strongly recommend you have personal travel insurance for your trip.
Can I take it outside Munich? +
Absolutely — that's half the fun. The EK3 is fully road-legal on German roads. Popular day trips include Starnberger See, Chiemsee, and Neuschwanstein. Just stay within Germany for the rental period.
How do I pay? +
After we confirm your booking, we send a Stripe payment link by email. You pay by card or PayPal. The €200 deposit is collected at pickup (cash or card) and returned immediately on clean return of the scooter.
What if something goes wrong? +
We're available by WhatsApp 24/7 during your rental. For mechanical issues, we'll come to you. If the scooter cannot be fixed quickly, you'll receive a pro-rated refund for unused days.
