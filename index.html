FAQ

Questions.

What driving licence do I need?+
For the Piaggio Zip (50cc): A standard car licence (Class B) is perfectly fine.

For the Horwin & Vespa (125cc): You need an A1, A2, A motorcycle licence, OR a B196 extension on your car licence. We verify your licence at handover.
How does fuel and charging work?+
The Horwin has a removable battery you can charge in your hotel room (5 hours for a full charge). The Vespa and Piaggio run on standard unleaded petrol (Super 95). You receive them full, please return them full.
Where do I pick up the scooter?+
We deliver right to your hotel, Airbnb, or any central Munich meeting point. We walk you through the controls in about 5 minutes and you're off.
Is the scooter insured?+
Yes. All vehicles carry the required German third-party liability insurance (Kfz-Haftpflicht). The €200 deposit covers minor damage on your end. We recommend carrying personal travel insurance as well.
How do I pay?+
After we confirm your request, we send a secure payment link by email (Stripe or PayPal). The €200 deposit is handled at handover — cash or card — and returned on clean return.
